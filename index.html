Why should you contribute poverty

Caring for the poor and needy people and helping them is a noble endeavor. The more you give to poor and needy people, the more you strengthen their dependency. If you give them the chance or opportunity, you'll see an effective and long-lasting improvement in their lives.

Millions of people are starving and forced to live a life in the absence of basic amenities. They do not have food, clothes, and homes to live safe and healthy. You can save somebody's life by making a donation that can help people in gaining basic amenities.

How can we help the needy

We can help the needy by:

Donating: One of the quickest and most obvious ways to help the world's poor is to donate to charity.

Talking to them: Giving them moral support and respect will make them feel that someone really does care about them.By helping them with oportunity and support them at every point.

Give some food: We can give leftovers to needy people on the streets so that we don't waste food and also help the needy.
